Tobacco

We have used Twitter to monitor attitudes towards tobacco products and smoking behavior. In particular, we have iteratively developed an annotation scheme to reflect important tobacco related categories (e.g., underage use, cessation attempts, marketing) and then used this annotation scheme to build a corpus of labeled tweets that can be used to train automatic NLP classifiers.
